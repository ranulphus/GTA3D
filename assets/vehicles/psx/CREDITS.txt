Used in GTA3D as the drivable vehicles. Each car is a single mesh with its wheels
modelled in; invisible VehicleWheel3D provide the physics. Colour/style variants
reuse one body mesh with a different .png skin (applied as a material override).

In the [C] cycle: Car 01/04/08 (4 colours each), Car 02/03 (3 each), Car 05
(taxi + police bodies). Car 06 is the burnt-out body, kept for a future
explosion/wreck feature (not in the cycle). Car 07 is omitted (not period
appropriate).

Only the .obj/.mtl/.png needed for rendering are vendored here; the full pack
(.blend sources, snow/extra colour variants, sound effects) is on the itch.io
page above. Attribution is not required under CC0, but credited here with thanks.
