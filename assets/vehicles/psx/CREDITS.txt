License: Creative Commons Zero v1.0 Universal (CC0 1.0) — public domain.

Used in GTA3D as the drivable vehicles. The car bodies (Car.obj .. Car8.obj) are
single meshes; the shared Wheel.obj is mounted on each wheel at runtime. Only the
.obj/.mtl/.png needed for rendering are vendored here; the full pack (.blend
sources, colour variants, sound effects) is on the itch.io page above.
